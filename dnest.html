D-NEST

  • D-NEST is a fully autonomous drone station powered by IOT and Machine Learning. A drone hub, which can house drones for continuous/scheduled monitoring or inspections. Through D-NEST industries can deploy the drone for inspection from anywhere across the globe to assess their assets (windmills, solar farms, power grids, dams and bridges) in remote locations.
  • The process is simple, they have to login and select one of the predefined inspection sequence(s). The drone, then takes off from the hub and will and starts collecting data. The drone do not need to be controlled, the system in the hub will take care of controlling the drone for the given mission.
  • We use custom algorithms to control drones using live visual feeds. The system in the hub is also responsible for processing the data acquired in parallel, so when the drone lands after the inspection the report of the entire session will be ready.
